How it works

  1. Click Start Practice to launch the quiz.
  2. Choose your question count (e.g., 20/30/50/100/200).
  3. Confirm the 15-minute timer and hit Start.
  4. Answer questions; you can skip, go back, and change answers anytime.
  5. When finished, see your score summary and review correct answers.
  6. Retake immediately to improve, or increase the question count for a tougher session.

Note: If you notice any issues or would like to provide feedback, please feel free to contact us. We're always open to hearing your thoughts and improving the experience for everyone. Thank you!.

About This Project

  • Purpose: Provide a realistic, stress-free way to practice the Lebanese driving theory exam.
  • Question Source: Based on officially distributed PDFs; wording is kept consistent for familiarity.
  • Timer & Flow: 15-minute sessions to mirror exam pressure, with optional higher question counts for training.
  • Randomization: Questions are shuffled every attempt to reduce memorization.
  • Device Friendly: Fully responsive for phones, tablets, and desktops with a clean, accessible UI.
  • Review Mode: End-of-session summary to check mistakes and learn quickly.
  • Privacy: No unnecessary data collection; practice results stay on your device/session.
  • Disclaimer: Training tool only — not affiliated with any government agency.

Learn More

Our Driving Test Quiz Application is a user friendly platform designed to help you confidently prepare for your driving test. With an easy to navigate website and straightforward content, it's perfect for all users.

Key Features:

  • Authentic Exam Timer: A fixed 15 minute timer, just like the real exam, helps users practice under real test conditions.
  • Multilingual Support: Users can choose up to 200 questions in three different languages English, Arabic, and French.
  • User-Friendly Design: The website is easy to navigate, with a clean layout available in three languages.
  • Randomized Question Order: Prevents memorization by shuffling questions each time a quiz is taken.
  • Exam Simulation Mode: A full length test that mimics real exam conditions. In the actual exam, you get 30 questions and need 24/30 to pass, but in our quiz, you can choose how many questions you want, and there's no failing it's purely for training to help you succeed.
  • Leaderboard & Scores: Compete with others and track high scores for motivation.
  • Simplified Questions: Easy to read and well structured questions to ensure effective preparation.
  • Success-Oriented Approach: Designed to boost knowledge and help users succeed in their exams.
